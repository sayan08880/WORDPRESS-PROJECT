CRT Addons for Elementor advanced widgets, templates, blocks. Builder header, footer, archive, every page.

== Description ==

CRT Addons for Elementor is a powerful enhancement to your page building experience, featuring a massive library of 50+ premium, highly-customizable widgets. From beautiful morphing blobs to advanced WooCommerce grids, this plugin gives you everything you need to build stunning themes.

### Included Widgets

**✨ Creative & Animations**
* **Blob Shapes**: 60fps morphing CSS shapes with gradients and blend modes.
* **Background Switcher**: GSAP-powered hover navigation that crossfades or slides backgrounds.
* **Scroll Marquee**: Infinite smooth-scrolling typography and images.
* **Lottie Animations**: JSON-based high-quality vector animations.

**🖼 Images & Media**
* **Advanced Slider**: Powerful image and content carousel.
* **Image Hotspots**: Place interactive tooltips anywhere over an image.
* **Before After**: Interactive slider to compare two images side-by-side.
* **Image Scroll**: Hover to automatically scroll through tall images/screenshots.
* **Video Playlist**: Curated lists of playable videos.
* **Logo**: Advanced logo displays.

**📝 Content & Text formatting**
* **Advanced Text** & **Dual Color Heading**: For beautiful, multi-layered, and dual-tone typography.
* **Data Tables**: Sortable, styled tables for complex data.
* **Advanced Accordion** & **Content Toggle**: Interactive content revealers and toggle switches.
* **Flip Box**: 3D animating interactive cards with front and back content.
* **Tabs**, **Content Ticker**, **Feature List**.

**🛒 E-Commerce & Dynamic Grids**
* **WooCommerce Builder**: Design fully customized Cart, Checkout, and My Account pages.
* **Woo Grid**: Advanced product displays with hover effects and quick views.
* **Magazine Grid**, **Media Grid**, and standard **Grid**: Complex masonry and CSS grids for posts and media.
* **Posts Timeline**: Display chronological content beautifully.
* **Category Grid** & **Taxonomy List**.

**🏗️ Theme Builder & Popups**
* **Dynamic Templates**: Visually build global Headers, Footers, and custom Archive Pages.
* **Popup Builder**: Create highly converting, advanced Popups for lead capture or announcements anywhere on the site.

**📱 Navigation & Structure**
* **Mega Menu** & **Nav Menu**: Comprehensive, responsive header navigation.
* **Offcanvas**: Slide-in sidebars for menus or cart items.
* **Reading Progress Bar** & **Back To Top**: UX enhancements for long pages.
* **Onepage Nav**: Smooth scrolling bullet navigation.

**📈 Marketing, Conversion & Social**
* **Form Builder** & **Forms**: Lead capture and complex form generation.
* **Mailchimp** Integration.
* **Testimonial Carousel**, **Promo Box**, **Pricing Table**, **Price List**.
* **Instagram Feed** & **Twitter Feed**: Live, customizable social media streams.
* **Countdown Timer** & **Business Hours**.
* **Phone Call**, **Button**, **Dual Button**, **Search**.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/crt-manage` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Open any page in the Elementor Editor and begin dragging and dropping the new CRT widgets onto your canvas.
